Hey there! My name is Annette, I started painting miniatures in 2016 in my freshman year of highschool

The first army that I got that got me into the army were Chaos Space Marines, in particular the World Eaters (My first mini pictured to the right)

The exansive lore and complete over the top grimdark aesthetic of the Warhammer universe immediately caught my attention and imagination

I kept painting throughout highschool, taking on commissions from friends and eventually coming to win a local painting tournament known as the Silver Squig

After graduating highschool and going to college, I ended up taking a year long hiatus, returning to paint after my first year of college

This leads to today, where I'm currently majoring in art and aspire to win the slayer sword while slowly working my way through my collection
